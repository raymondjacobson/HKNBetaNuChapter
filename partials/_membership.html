Membership in HKN is by invitation only. Eligible students are drawn from the top 1/4 of juniors and the top 1/3 of seniors in the ECSE Department. Membership is for life.

The benefits of membership in HKN are many and include recognition of exceptional academic achievement, opportunities to develop organizational and leadership skills, and access to an industry-wide network of HKN members and those who recognize membership as a mark of accomplishment.

For more information, feel free to contact any of the Officers or visit the website of the HKN Headquarters, where you can find video testimonials from RPI's own Ken Connor and others.
